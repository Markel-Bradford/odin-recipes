Fruit Punch Pops

Description

Is there anything more refreshing on a hot summer day than a tasty glass of fruit punch? What about a fruit punch popsicle? Sounds amazing, right? If so, it's your lucky day because I will be teaching you how you can make this delicious and refreshing treat at home! These popsicles are a great summer treat for kids and adults alike.

To make these sweet treats, the ingredient list is quite simple. Ginger ale, pineapple juice, apple juice, strawberries, and pineapple chunks. Really, that's all!If you can obtain these Ingredients then you are one step closer to making a delicious fruit punch mixture for fruit punch popsicles.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ginger ale
  • 2 1/2 cups pineapple juice
  • 2 cups apple juice
  • 16oz strawberries
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks, drained

Directions

  1. Pour room temperature ginger ale into a bowl. Let sit for two hours or until soda is flat.
  2. In a large pitcher, combine apple juice and pineapple juice.
  3. Very carefully, stir the flat ginger ale in with the apple and pineapple juice.
  4. Pour punch into popsicle molds (Pssst...! You can buy some here!)
  5. Enjoy!
  6. Freeze overnight or until popsicles are frozen solid.
